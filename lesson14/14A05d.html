CANADA

Canada is a country in the northern part of North America. Its ten provinces and three territories extend from the Atlantic to the Pacific and northward into the Arctic Ocean, covering 9.98 million square kilometres with the United States, stretching some 8,891 kilometres (5,521 mi), is the world's longest bi-national land border. Canada's capital its Ottawa, and its largest metropolitian areas are Toronto, Montreal, and Vancouver.
