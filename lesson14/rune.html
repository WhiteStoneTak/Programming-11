Rune Factory 4 Review

Journey to the vibrant world of Rune Factory and experience the legendary fantasy farming adventure like never before!!-Nintendo Switch Online

Metacritie: 82%

Nintendo Life: 9/10

Rune Factory 4 Special is an interesting case as I didn't think I would enjoy this game as much i did. The game is essentially life sim + farm sim + dating sim. It's a very versatile game that includes every single bit that makes series like Animal Crossing or Harvest Moon great. The combat is surprisingly entertaining and the cast is all-around lovely people. The only problem is that you can tell this game was not made recently but rather ported. Rf4 was originally a 3DS title and hence, it is understandable if you find the art style a little old or jarring looking. Also, the voice acting is great but rather sparse and limited. In celebration that Rune Factory 5 is coming, I thought I would try out 4 and wasn't disappointed. 8/10 for me!!
