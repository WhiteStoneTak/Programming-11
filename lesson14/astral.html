Astral Chain Review

Master multiple combat styles in this synergetic action game!-Nintendo Switch Online

Metacritie: 85%

Nintendo Life: 9/10

Platinum has been known for making high-quality games (Bayonetta, Devil May Cry) but suffers from poor sales despite every title getting a dedicated fanbase. Astral Chain is a hack-and-slash action game where you play as a cop who fight and investigate with creatures called "Legion" - a humanoid monster that are chained to your arm. Combat is you and your Legion working together so it can get a little tedious (the game offers great tutorial however). The mechanics incorporate Legion heavily but it does not depend on the player too much. The storyline is pretty basic and slightly predictable but the extra contents (life- sim, investigating) makes it worth it. A great game despite lack of replay ability. 8/10 for me!!!
