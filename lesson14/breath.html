The Legend of Zelda- Breath of The Wild Review

Step into a world of adventure!-Nintendo Switch Online

Metacritie: 95%

Nintendo Life: 9/10

Zelda has been a cult classic for many people, myself not included. However, BOTW looked too good to pass so I gave it a shot and was not let down. The graphics looks magical and was simply breath-taking. There are many mechanics in which you explore, climb mountains or gather herbs. Don't have much too say but it's a great game. Honestly glad that this is my first Zelda game. Looking forward to BOTW2! 7/10 for me!!!!
